Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Chocolate chip cookies are a traditional favorite. These cookies are great to have as a snack, to make with your kids, or share with family and friends. These cookies are best with the company of a tall glass of milk or a friend. You really can't go wrong with bringing chocolate chip cookies to a party.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ tsp. baking soda
  • ¾ tsp. salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • ¾ cup light brown sugar
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 Tbsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups chocolate chips or chunks

Instructions

  1. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl.
  2. Combine butter and sugars using a mixer. Beat on medium-low for 5 minutes until mixture is fluffy.
  3. Add egg and vanilla. Beat until well combined.
  4. Gradually add flour, continue to beat on low until the all of the flour is combined.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop 1 ½ Tbsp. of dough into balls and place on baking sheet 2 inches apart.
  7. Chill the dough in the freezer for 30 minutes or let rest overnight in the fridge.
  8. Preheat the oven to 375° F.
  9. Bake cookies for 10 to 12 minutes.
  10. Let cookies cool on a wire rack for 5 to 10 minutes.
