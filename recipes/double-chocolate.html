Double Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

If you absolutely love chocolate, then you definitely need these cookies in your life. They are the perfect snack for every chocoholic!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp. baking soda
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ½ unsalted butter softened
  • ½ cup light brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips or chocolate chunks

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large mixing bowl beat butter and sugars on medium speed until light and fluffy.
  4. Add egg and vanilla and egg, beat until combined.
  5. Mixing on low speed slowly add the flour mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop 1 ½ Tbsp. of dough into balls and place on baking sheet 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake cookies for 10 to 12 minutes.
  9. Let cookies cool on a wire rack for 5 to 10 minutes.
