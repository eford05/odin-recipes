Sugar Cookies Recipes

These cookies are rich, buttery, and kissed with just a pinch of vanilla. They soft and chewy with just enough crunch to the edges. They will melt in your mouth and melt your heart.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • ¾ tsp. salt
  • ¾ tsp. baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extra

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, salt, and baking powder.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugar together on medium speed for two minutes.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time while mixing.
  4. Still mixing gradually add the flour to the mixture until well incorporated.
  5. Divide the dough into two. Wrap both with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours and up to 2 days.
  6. Remove dough from the fridge and let stand until it reaches room temperature.
  7. Roll the dough on lightly floured surface and cut shapes using a cookie cutter then place on a lined baking sheet 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake cookies at 375°F. for 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool a little bit on the sheet before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
